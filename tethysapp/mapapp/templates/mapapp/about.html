About

February 26th, 2018


Jorge Luis Sanchez-Lozano is a Civil Engineering PhD Student at Brigham Young University. He was born in La Dorada, Caldas - Colombia.

He loves football (wrong called soccer in the US), and he supports the biggest team ever in the football history: Club Atletico Nacional de Colombia.

Jorge Luis finished his high school at Colegio Departamental Mixto de Puerto Salgar, in Puerto Salgar, Cundinamarca - Colombia. After that, he moved to Bogota, where he did his bachelor in Agricultural Engineering and his Master Degree in Water Resources at Universidad Nacional de Colombia - Sede Bogota.

Jorge Luis created mapapp, as part of the course 514-Geospatial software development. If you have any question about the app, you can write an email to jorgessanchez7@gmail.com
